Smart resize

Your product always fills
the canvas edge-to-edge

Most tools either crop your product or leave it floating with ugly empty borders. PixelPrep scales your image to fill the canvas as much as possible while preserving the full aspect ratio — nothing ever gets cropped.

The rule: Portrait images (taller than wide) fill top & bottom edges first. Landscape images (wider than tall) fill left & right edges first. Either way, your product touches at least two edges — it never floats in the middle. Your chosen background colour fills the remaining space.

Why file size matters

Smaller images =
faster store = more sales

Page speed directly affects conversion rates. A 1-second delay in page load time can reduce conversions by up to 7%. PixelPrep produces the smallest possible JPEG at the best visual quality — so your product pages load fast on every connection.

4:4:4
Full chroma subsampling
Preserves colour detail at every pixel. Most tools use 4:2:0 which halves colour resolution and makes product edges look muddy.
Q85
Optimal quality setting
Quality 85 is the sweet spot — visually indistinguishable from Q95 but 30–40% smaller file size. Beyond Q85, you pay in bytes without visible benefit.
Progressive
Renders top-to-bottom
Progressive JPEGs render a low-res preview immediately, then sharpen as they load. Products appear instantly on slow connections instead of loading line by line.
