Smart Resize
Your image is scaled to fill the canvas edge-to-edge — top & bottom first, sides if needed. Nothing is ever cropped. White (or your chosen colour) fills any remaining space.
🤖
AI Background Removal
When enabled, our AI (BiRefNet) isolates your product and places it on your chosen background colour. Works best on clear product shots. Complex images with fine edges may vary in accuracy.
🗜️
Optimised Compression
Every image is saved as a progressive JPEG with EXIF metadata stripped, 4:4:4 colour subsampling, and Huffman-optimised encoding — smallest file size at highest visual quality.
🔒
Privacy
Your images are processed on our server and immediately discarded. We do not store, retain, or use your images for any purpose after processing is complete.
⚠️ Disclaimers
  • AI background removal works best on product photos with a clear, solid background. Images with complex scenes, hair, fur, or transparent objects may produce imperfect results.
  • Interior gaps within products may not be fully removed by AI — this is a known limitation of automated background removal.
  • Always review your processed images before publishing. We recommend keeping your original files as a backup.
  • PixelPrep is a tool to assist your workflow — final image quality decisions remain your responsibility.
